Non-profit Partnership "Applied Research Center for Computer Networks" (ARCCN) is a research project created to develop technologies and products for computer networks of the new generation in Russian Federation. We develop and implement the latest promising technologies in the area of computer networks and the Internet, demonstrate and verify the effectiveness of these technologies on the challenges of industry and business.
